Social Media Optimization

Modern age brands and companies are more entwined with audiences than ever. From the Wendy’s twitter to Gillete making social justice ad campaigns, brands are seeking connection to audiences through social media and the internet as a whole. To help generate more clicks on these platforms, understanding Social Media Optimization (or SMO) is key. Investopedia defines SMO as, “the use of social media networks to manage and grow an organization’s message and online presence.” This idea is incredibly important to raising awareness of your brand or product.

It’s not a new concept, but it’s become tied to a sister concept, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), which is the art of manipulating search engines to show your website above others. These used to be separate concepts, but now they’re increasingly married together.

In fact, a survey by Searchengineland found that 38% of companies that had successful SEO campaigns also had a large social media standing. All of this is part of viral marketing. A strategy revolving around fast spreading word of mouth advertising stimulated by a conversation online. Love or hate Gillete you know about their “Men” video. That’s a successful viral marketing campaign. And the internet, especially social media, is a great outlet for this.

An advantage of online marketing, and SMO as an extension, is that it’s almost instant and extremely cheap. Every retweet of a Wendy’s tweet, message about Nike supporting Colin Kaepernick, or post about loving/hating the Gillete ad is free marketing. It’s extremely efficient and cheap to create or spread a social media campaign. This optimization is beneficial for any business, especially smaller startups. The cost of running a twitter page is low, it reaches a broad audience, and it allows for engagement with that audience.

The optimization of getting your brand recognized is fairly similar to search engine optimization, but now involves personal touches. Creating a strong profile, original content, images, hashtags and the like will boost your brand on the platform, and in turn boost the likelihood that you are topping the google results.
