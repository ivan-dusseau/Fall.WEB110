Audience, Issues and Approach (AIA)

Client's name and the final decision maker Sofie Friedlander, Girlfriend
Client's business (website) name ScrappedReeds
Redesign or brand new website? NEW Website
Type of website Information, Social Networking, Training
Client's goal? Compile a comprehensive list of bassoon reed styles with information on the production and fine tuning of each kind.
Audiences Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Students: Aspiring bassoonists of all ages looking to improve their technique with reeds, or develop more than one technique. Browsing from home, most likely on a mac laptop screen. Most likely using safari, and fairly comfortable with it. Possibly even using phone or tablet screen.
  • Needs:
  • Clear Examples
  • Step by step guides
  • Tips and tricks
  • Inspiration
  • Other resources
  • Issues/Concerns:
  • Poor materials
  • Bad basics
  • Lots of example images
  • In depth explanations of concepts
  • Links to outside resources
  • Personal recommendations
  • FAQ
Teachers: Teachers looking for a resource to share with students and expand their horizons. Mostly using large projector screens with low resolution, internet explorer and old windows.
  • Needs:
  • Example material
  • Easy to follow guides
  • Class ready material
  • Issues/Concerns:
  • Non-vetted methods
  • Too much for students to learn at once

 
  • Sourcing from professional methods
  • Sectioned material
  • Beginner pages
Bassoonists: Hobbyists or players just looking into other forms of reed making or processes to improve their own technique. The most general category, probably on desktop through safari or firefox. Most likey using mac.
  • Needs::
  • Not too in depth
  • Easy to follow
  • Simple instructions
  • Issues/Concerns:
  • Retention
  • Loss of interest
  • Sectioned content
  • Step by step instructions
  • Basic examples with more in depth notes
